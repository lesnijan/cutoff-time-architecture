Glossary

Key terms used in this documentation

Business Terms

Cutoff Time

The deadline by which orders can be accepted with a promise of "same-day" shipping. After this time, new orders are processed the next business day.

Same-Day Delivery

Delivery on the same day. The percentage of orders shipped on the day they were placed. Key KPI for warehouse operations.

Workload

The sum of labor required for all orders waiting to be fulfilled. Expressed in time units or conventional work units.

Capacity

Maximum amount of work the warehouse can perform per unit of time. Depends on staff count, process efficiency and resource availability.

Utilization

The ratio of current workload to capacity, expressed as a percentage. UTILIZATION = WORKLOAD / CAPACITY √ó 100%

Technical Terms

SAP S/4HANA

SAP's integrated ERP system. Source of data on orders, deliveries and warehouse resources.

SAP BTP

SAP Business Technology Platform - cloud platform for building and hosting applications.

CDS Views

Core Data Services - semantic layer in SAP HANA. Defines data views with business logic.

C4 Model

A method for documenting software architecture at 4 levels: Context, Container, Component, Code.
